Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 1, 2025

Welcome to VibeScale (“we”, “our”, “us”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application, VibeScale (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process only the minimum information necessary to provide and improve our services:

  • User-provided data: Such as your song notes, chord charts, lyrics, or any audio you upload or record within the app.
  • Account information: If you sign in (e.g., via Google, Apple, or email), we store your basic identifier (name, email, user ID) to enable synchronization.
  • Device information: Basic technical data (device model, operating system, app version) for performance optimization and bug tracking.
  • Crash and analytics data: Anonymous usage metrics (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics) to maintain app stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the app’s core features, such as saving, syncing, and organizing your musical content.
  • To back up and restore your data securely across devices.
  • To fix bugs, improve usability, and ensure performance.
  • To communicate important updates or changes (only if you opt in).

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data may be stored using secure cloud infrastructure (such as Firebase). We use encryption in transit (HTTPS) and authentication controls to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure; please keep your account credentials confidential.

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.
  • We only share data with trusted third-party providers (e.g., Firebase) strictly for app functionality.
  • We may disclose data if required by law or to enforce our rights.

5. Your Choices

  • You can delete your content anytime inside the app.
  • You can request full account deletion by contacting us at vibescale.app@gmail.com.
  • You can opt out of optional analytics in future versions when available.

6. Permissions Used

  • Microphone: For recording audio clips within the app.
  • Storage/Media: To save or attach audio and chord data locally.
  • We do not access location, contacts, or sensitive device identifiers.

7. Children’s Privacy

VibeScale is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in this document with a revised “Last updated” date. Significant updates will be communicated via app notifications or the Play Store listing.
